Books I read in 2018

Posted by Rohan Adhyapak on October 31, 2017

Reading is one of the habits that is increasingly becoming difficult to enforce in today's world of technology. The way I see it, reading books enables me to have more control over my time and helps me maintain a modicum of daily internet interaction. It is very easy to get lost in the world wide web, with billions of dollars being poured into developing flamboyant user interfaces with the sole goal of giving the end user the ultimate user experience, it it easy to sometimes lose oneself on the internet. Reading for me is what helps me control this so that I dont spend too much time simply browsing the internet. Here are the books I read in 2018:

The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho

Train to Pakistan by Khuswant Singh

The Firm by John Grisham
