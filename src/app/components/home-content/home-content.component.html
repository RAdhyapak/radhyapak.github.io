My Story

The journey till now

I was born in the city of Guwahati in India. It was back in 2001, when I first crossed paths with a personal computer. My father bought these set of boxes that were connected by wires with a screen on one of them, where you could draw with virtual paint and play games like solitaire and minesweeper. After using it for a few days, I no longer had to fight with my brother for the control of the television. I had found something else to dig my head in. I soon got hold of a Britannica Encyclopedia CD, and played the quiz game over and over, so many times that I knew the answers even before reading the questions. Soon enough I got introduced to the programming language of Java as part of the school curriculum and all I used to wonder is, how simple instructions that we were writing in Java could be actually used to create complex softwares that I used to run on my computer back home. At the time, it seemed improbable.

With the increasing love of computers, I moved to Vellore to pursue Bachelors in Computer Science. Motivated with the zeal to learn about the craft of software making, I dug deeply into everything I learnt and gradutated with the degree of B.Tech in Computer Science.

I had got placed into one of the biggest companies in travel technology and got the opportunity to undergo internship during last semester of my college. During this period I simultanepusly worked on my final year project. My project guide had recently started working on initiatives in the smart city mission, which is a push by the Government of India to modernise cities across India.
